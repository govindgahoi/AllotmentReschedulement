The undersigned hereby approves the building plan submitted by you with following terms & conditions:-

  • This permission is being given in accordance to the Uttar Pradesh State Industrial Development Authority Land and Building Regulations 2018. This permission neither seeks to effect favourably/ unfavourably the existing ownership rights nor gives any additional ownership rights in anyway. This approval neither affirms/ nor controverts any litigated issue
  • If it is revealed that this approval has been obtained by submitting invalid/ non-genuine and false facts or documents, the approval shall be liable to be cancelled. Further the Authority shall be free to take up suitable legal action.
  • Any proposal for construction over public road either in shape of stone or by constructing any arcade, balcony, cornice, chhajja or any other such construction if shown in the building plan shall not be deemed to be considered as approved simultaneously. Such proposal shall require specific approval of the concerned Municipal body/ Development Authority under its act & provision.
  • If any change/alternation is sought in the approved building plan then the allottee shall be required to submit a copy of this approval along with application for change/ alteration.
  • The approval does not give any right to the allottee or its authorized representative to make any construction on the public premises or to make any construction over or under the area through which an/the electricity line is passing unless and until such electricity has been removed by concerned power authorities.
  • Mandatory green plantation, Parking provisions, Rain water harvesting and Anti Earthquake provisions as per norms of Authority/Govt, shall have to be developed and maintained by the allottee inside the plot.
  • The Allottee shall strictly abide by the terms & conditions laid down by the Fire Department while granting NOC.
  • The Authority reserves the right to demand any dues with regard to the layout approval if a balance/dues are discovered identified anytime in future. The allottee shall be liable to deposit the same within the time allowed by the Authority.
  • The allottee shall have to obtain and submit NOC of Pollution Department before starting production inthe plot, failing which the building plan shall be treated cancelled automatically.
  • The Allottee/Architect/ Engineer shall have to comply mandatorily with the provisions of Preperation and Finalisation of Plan Regulations-2004, Uttar Pradesh Industrial Area Development Act, 1976 (U.P. Act 6 of1976),The Uttar Pradesh State Industrial Development Area-Land Development & Building Regulations-2018, National Construction Code, B.I.S. Standards & Bye Laws of concerned Authority. They shall be solely responsible for abiding by the changes made from time to time in aboverules and regulations and shall keep UPSIDA/ UPSIDC indemnified against any breach committed by them.
  • Officers/ Officials of the Authoritys/ UPSIDA shall have the right to make site inspection anytime during construction.
  • In case of any breach the concerning Engineer/Architect shall be liable to be blacklisted. Further the Authority may even consider cancellation of allotment.
  • This Approval shall be valid till the time allocated mentioned in the lease deed for bringing the unit under production or for an extended period as duly approved by the Authority.
